Privacy Policy for brownpaw

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to brownpaw ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name through Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In.
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details you choose to provide.
  • Logbook Entries: River descent logs including dates, river runs, flow levels, ratings, notes, and photos you upload.
  • Favorites: Lists of river runs you mark as favorites.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: We collect your approximate location (when permission is granted) to show nearby river runs on the map. Location data is only used while you're actively using the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and screens viewed.
  • Log Data: Technical logs including crash reports and performance data.

2.3 Third-Party Data

  • River Level Data: We retrieve publicly available river level and flow data from Environment Canada's hydrometric monitoring network.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain the brownpaw app, including your personal logbook, favorites, and river level information.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience, such as showing nearby river runs and your favorite locations.
  • Communication: To send you service-related notifications, updates, and important announcements.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve app features and performance.
  • Safety: To monitor for fraudulent activity and ensure the security of our services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database storage, cloud storage, and hosting.
  • Google Maps: For displaying map features and location services.
  • Apple Sign-In: For authentication services.

4.3 Public Information

You can choose to make certain logbook entries public. Public entries may be visible to other brownpaw users. By default, all logbook entries are private.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or governmental request.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS and at rest using Firebase's security standards.
  • Firebase Security: We use Google Firebase with strict security rules to protect your data from unauthorized access.
  • Location: Data is stored on Firebase servers, which may be located in various countries. Firebase complies with applicable data protection regulations.
  • Offline Cache: The app caches data locally on your device for offline access. This data is stored securely on your device.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Logbook Entries: Retained until you delete them or close your account.
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 90 days for analytics and improvement purposes.
  • Account Deletion: You can request account deletion at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, your personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Correction

You can view and edit your profile information and logbook entries through the app at any time.

7.2 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a portable format by contacting us.

7.3 Deletion

You can delete individual logbook entries or request full account deletion through the app settings.

7.4 Location Permissions

You can enable or disable location access in your device settings. The app will still function without location access, but map features will be limited.

7.5 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

8. Children's Privacy

brownpaw is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

The app may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., navigation apps, external river guides). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending you a notification through the app or email for material changes

Your continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. International Users

brownpaw is operated from Canada and primarily serves users in Canada (British Columbia and Alberta). If you access the app from outside Canada, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in Canada and other countries where our service providers operate.

12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
